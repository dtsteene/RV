## Main Structural Risk

The thesis has the right material, but the boundaries between theory, numerical methods, implementation, and experiments are not perfectly clean. This is acceptable if the chapter introductions explain why the material is split this way. The model subfiles are now nested under the model overview in `myst.yml`, so they should read more like sections of one model chapter than separate top-level chapters. The reader still needs to know that:

1. `The Scientific Question` is the mathematical framing of the comparison.
2. `The Computational Model` gives the continuous and discrete model ingredients.
3. `Implementation and Challenges` explains what had to be changed, debugged, and verified in software.
4. `Calibrating the Circulation Model` is part of the experimental setup, not a separate clinical claim about PAH progression.
